Verify that some method calls are imported statically. At the moment, as per BCP bank rules, this looks for:
  • Assertions methods (like Assertions.assertTrue )
  • Mockito general methods (like Mockito.when() )
  • Mockito matching methods (like Mockito.isA() )
  • Mockito's any() method which is not allowed

Warning: This plugin was made to work exclusively with the codebase of the BCP bank of Peru.
